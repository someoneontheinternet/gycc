The GYCC Online Judge

Online Judge

Global Youth Coding Cup (GYCC) Online Judge is a platform for coders to practice computer programming. It provides programming challenges for practice and hosts GYCC programming contests.

Question Format

The question involves basic to advanced level of knowledge on programming language, data structure, and algorithms. It ranges from basic knowledge of string, array, recursion, sorting, to more advanced algorithms and mathematical reasoning.

What We Do

GYCC aims to provide the most fun computer programming contest for elementary and secondary school students. It provides a series of programming challenges in an exciting, motivating, and competitive environment. It is a valuable opportunity for young students to test their abilities of designing and implementing methods and algorithms.
